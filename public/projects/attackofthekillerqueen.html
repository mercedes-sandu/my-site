"Attack of the Killer Queen" is a song from the Deltarune 2's incredible soundtrack. Although I have yet to play the game, I was immediately drawn to this song when I listened to the full soundtrack while studying physical chemistry at the beginning of my sophomore year of university. The upbeat tempo and unique, catchy melody remained stuck in my head for days, and I knew I had to arrange it for piano.
